The residents of the fantastic world of Gensokyo have woken up to find that the very fabric of existence has been distorted! Polished diamonds are growing on trees, dirt paths are now made of fancy glass, landmarks have changed positions, and most disturbingly, even people seem to be behaving differently. To find out what's happening, the defenders of Gensokyo must explore a surreal new world and maybe even learn to bend the rules of reality themselves...

Analogue Reverie's first released project, Dream Logical World is a Touhou Project fan game. Styled after the official Touhou shoot-'em-up games, this adventure will have you shooting down enemies and bosses while dodging complex attacks, but with a strategic twist—in place of traditional bombs, you'll harness the fluctuating nature of existence with reality-breaking Aspect Shifts. To survive to the end of the game and rack up high scores, you'll need to master your chosen characters' Aspect Shifts and keep them active for as long as you can.

Dream Logical World features five difficulty levels, detailed practice options to help you craft your strategies, and if you're interested in lore, a story in which every separate campaign weaves together with mysteries and twists along the way.

東方再会 ~ Remembrance of Past Prayers

(Touhou Saikai ~ Remembrance of Past Prayers, lit. "Eastern Reunion")

Genre: Shoot-'em-up (Bullet Hell)
Engine: Game Maker
Project lead: WishMakers

Remembrance of Past Prayers is an 8-bit fan-made Touhou Project game that takes heavy inspiration from Raizing STGs. This game was conceived during the Hakurei Cleaning Game Jam event and has been on hiatus since.

Currently, the game is low priority on the development list, but the whole soundtrack has been completed and is available to listen to.
